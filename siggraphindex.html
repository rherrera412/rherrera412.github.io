SIGGRAPH:

Student Chapter

Goal:

Set up a community of computer graphics enthusiasts, from all fields (be it computer science, art, film). Promote an academic, artistic, and educational perspective of computer graphics. Groups involved in CG filmmaking, VR, and game development can benefit from events and resources organized by the chpater.

Set up 3D workshops, socials, film screenings/festivals, studio tours, guest speakers, SIGGRAPH Volunteer, networking.

Foster an expanding community in Berkeley, that reaches out to lower and upper division students, and maintains a long lasting presense in Berkeley and the rest of the Bay.

SIGGRAPH is a special interests group for computer graphics, holding yearly conferences for CG in all fields.
