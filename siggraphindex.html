ACM SIGGRAPH:

Student Chapter

Goal:

Set up a community of computer graphics enthusiasts, from all fields (be it computer science, art, film). A community for networking.

Set up 3D workshops, socials, film screenings/festivals, studio tours, guest speakers, SIGGRAPH Volunteer, networking.

Foster an expanding community in Berkeley and the rest of the Bay.
