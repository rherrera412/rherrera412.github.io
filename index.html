FILMS

The Only Love a Mans Ever Known (exp. 2017)
This project was made for UC Berkeley Advanced Digital Animation course, CNM190. In essence, its a short about the pangs of alcoholism, but that is all I can say for now. I worked on audio, lighting, modeling, and post-production for this short. It has won an award at the East LA film festival, and is being considered for other film festivals!
